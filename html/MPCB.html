Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB)

1) Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA):

Preparation of EIA reports: Consultants help in preparing Environmental Impact Assessment reports for projects that may significantly impact the environment.
Clearance and permissions: Consultants assist in obtaining environmental clearances from the MPCB and other regulatory bodies.

2) Consent to Establish (CTE):

Consultants help industries and businesses in applying for Consent to Establish (CTE) from the MPCB.
They assist in preparing and submitting all required documentation for obtaining CTE, which is required for setting up any new industrial plant.

3)Consent to Operate (CTO):

Once a facility is set up, MPCB consultants assist in obtaining the Consent to Operate (CTO) for the business to legally operate.
Consultants ensure that the facility complies with all environmental standards and submit the necessary reports to MPCB for renewal or issuance of CTO.

4)Environmental Clearance & Permissions

Consultants assist in obtaining the necessary environmental clearances from the relevant authorities such as the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) or State Environmental Impact Assessment Authority (SEIAA).
This includes permissions for construction, mining, and other activities that could affect the environment.

5)Effluent and Emission Monitoring

Consultants help in setting up systems to monitor and control effluents and emissions in industries to ensure compliance with the MPCB standards.
They provide support in submitting reports regarding air, water, and noise pollution as part of regulatory requirements.

6)Environmental Management Plan (EMP)

Consultants assist in formulating an Environmental Management Plan (EMP) to minimize the impact of pollution from operations.
This includes recommendations for pollution control systems (e.g., sewage treatment plants, air scrubbers, etc.) and strategies for waste management.

7)Waste Management Services

Solid Waste Management: Providing solutions for the proper disposal and recycling of industrial waste. Hazardous Waste Management: Handling the disposal of hazardous and biomedical waste in compliance with the Hazardous Waste Management Rules. E-Waste Management: Offering guidance on safe disposal and recycling of electronic waste.

8) Environmental Audits and Compliance

Consultants conduct environmental audits to ensure that industries are compliant with the MPCB's environmental regulations. They provide gap analysis, suggest improvements, and help in complying with legal requirements, including periodic audits and inspections.

9)Pollution Control Equipment Design and Installation

Consultants design and install pollution control systems like air pollution control devices (APCD), sewage treatment plants (STP), effluent treatment plants (ETP), and water treatment systems to ensure industry compliance with environmental standards.

10)Pollution Load Calculation

They calculate the pollution load generated by an industry (air, water, waste) and suggest necessary measures to control it according to MPCB regulations.

11)Occupational Health and Safety (OHS)

Consultants provide support in ensuring a safe working environment by adhering to environmental health and safety standards. They may assist in implementing occupational health and safety measures such as regular health check-ups, noise, and air quality monitoring for workers.

12) Noise Pollution Control

Consultants evaluate and monitor noise pollution levels in industrial operations and suggest measures to reduce noise pollution to comply with permissible limits set by the MPCB.

13)Renewal of Consents

Consultants help in the renewal of CTE and CTO, ensuring compliance with environmental norms during the operational phase of industries. They ensure the necessary environmental documentation is up-to-date for successful renewal.

14)Environmental Training & Awareness

Conducting training and workshops for organizations to educate them about environmental laws, pollution control techniques, and the importance of sustainability. They may provide specialized training on waste management, water conservation, and compliance with environmental norms.

15)Legal Services and Representation

Consultants can assist with legal advice related to environmental matters, including navigating disputes with the MPCB or other government authorities. They may also represent businesses in legal proceedings related to environmental non-compliance.

16)Sustainability and CSR Initiatives

Consultants assist businesses in adopting sustainable practices, reducing their carbon footprint, and meeting Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) goals related to environmental protection. They may guide companies in implementing eco-friendly practices such as energy efficiency, water conservation, and waste recycling.

17)Green Building Certification

Consultants assist in getting green building certifications (like LEED or IGBC) by helping industries comply with environmental norms related to energy usage, waste management, and water conservation.

18)Pollution Source Identification & Reduction Strategies

Identification of potential sources of pollution within an industrial setup and providing recommendations for minimizing pollution through process modifications or technology upgrades.
