BIS (License and Annual Renewal)

1) BIS License Application Assistance:

Comprehensive support to businesses in obtaining a BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) license for compliance with quality and safety standards.

  • Documentation Preparation: Assist in gathering and organizing the required documents for the application.
  • Product Certification: Guide businesses in selecting the appropriate BIS certification scheme based on their product category.
  • Testing and Compliance: Coordinate with BIS-approved laboratories for product testing to ensure compliance with relevant standards.
  • Application Submission: Ensure accurate and timely submission of the BIS license application.

2) BIS Certification Process Guidance:

Navigate businesses through the BIS certification process, ensuring successful approval.

  • Initial Audit Preparation: Prepare the company for BIS factory audits, including training staff and reviewing documentation.
  • Inspection and Testing Coordination: Schedule and manage product inspections and testing as part of the certification process.
  • Technical Query Resolution: Address queries or discrepancies raised during the BIS evaluation process.

3) Annual Renewal Services:

Streamlined services for renewing BIS licenses to ensure continued compliance.

  • Document Updates: Review and update required documentation for the renewal process.
  • Compliance Verification: Conduct an internal audit to confirm ongoing adherence to BIS standards.
  • Renewal Application Filing: Timely submission of renewal applications with all necessary information.
  • Fee Management: Assist with calculating and paying renewal fees to avoid lapses.

4) Post-Certification Support:

Continuous assistance to maintain compliance and address any issues related to BIS certification.

  • Surveillance Audits: Support during periodic BIS surveillance audits to ensure standards are upheld.
  • Non-Compliance Rectification: Resolve issues identified during audits or inspections promptly.
  • Standard Updates: Inform clients of any updates or changes in BIS standards relevant to their products.

5) Industry-Specific Guidance:

Tailored support for different industries to comply with BIS requirements effectively.

  • Electrical and Electronics: Assistance with certifications like ISI and CRS for electronic products.
  • Food and Beverages: Ensure compliance with BIS standards for food safety and packaging.
  • Construction Materials: Certification for cement, steel, and other construction products.
